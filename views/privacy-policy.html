Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 12, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide and improve our services, including:

  • Personal Identification Data: Name, Email Address, and Password (stored as a secure hash).
  • Order Details: Assignment instructions, academic level, pages, deadlines, and attached files.
  • Financial Data: Wallet balance and transaction history (Note: We do not store full credit card details; all payments are processed securely via Cashapp and Zelle).
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, and interaction logs for security and service optimization.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly to fulfill the contract of service:

  • To create and manage your user account.
  • To process and fulfill your orders by matching you with a qualified writer.
  • To process payments and manage your wallet balance.
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our platform.
  • To communicate with you regarding your orders and provide customer support.

3. Confidentiality and Security

We are deeply committed to protecting your academic and personal privacy.

  • All user-provided instructions and documents related to orders are treated with 100% confidentiality.
  • We employ industry-standard security measures, including HTTPS, secure hashing for passwords (bcrypt), and CSRF protection, to safeguard your data.
  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third-party services, and your data may be shared with them only to the extent necessary to operate the service:

  • Payment Gateway (Zelle / Cashapp): Used to process all financial transactions.
  • Cloud Hosting (VPS): Used to securely host the application and user data.
  • Analytics: We may use basic, anonymized tools to monitor website traffic.
